Welcome to the Experiment

In this experiment, you will repeatedly make decisions involving a public project.

Your Endowment

At the beginning of each round, you will receive 10 coins. These coins do not carry over between rounds. Each round starts with a new endowment of 10 coins.

Your Decision

In every round, you must decide how many of your 10 coins (between 0 and 10) you would like to contribute to a public project.

How the Public Project Works

The public project will be provided only if the total contribution of the three participants in your interaction is at least 15 coins.

  • If total contributions are 15 coins or more:
    • The total contribution will be doubled.
    • The doubled amount will be divided equally among the three participants.
  • If total contributions are less than 15 coins:
    • The public project will not be provided.
    • All contributed coins are lost.

Your Payoff in a Round

If the threshold is reached:

Your payoff = 10 − Your Contribution + (2 × Total Contribution ÷ 3)

If the threshold is NOT reached:

Your payoff = 10 − Your Contribution

Important

  • All decisions are anonymous.
  • Your payoff depends on your decision and the decisions of the other participants.
  • Communication rules (if any) will be explained before each decision.

On the next page, you will answer a short quiz to ensure you understand the rules. You must answer all questions correctly before proceeding.
