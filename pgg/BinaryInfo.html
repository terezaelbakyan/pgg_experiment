Message to Your Group Members

Before making your contribution decision each round, you will have the opportunity to indicate how many coins you intend to contribute to the public project.

Your stated intend of contribution will be visible to all members of your group. You will also be able to see the stated intend of contributions of your group members.

This message is non-binding. Your actual contribution decision is made separately and can be any amount between 0 and 10 coins.

You receive 10 coins each round. The project is provided if total contributions reach 15 coins. If provided, contributions are doubled and shared equally. If not, contributions are lost.
