Free Communication Instructions

You will have 90 seconds to communicate with the other members of your group.

Before anyone makes a contribution decision, your group has a 90-second discussion period, during which you are allowed to send messages to the other members of your group and vice versa. The messages you send are seen by both other members of your group.

The messages must conform to the following rules:

  1. Your messages must be relevant to the experiment. Do not engage in social chat.
  2. You are not permitted to send messages intended to reveal your identity.
  3. The use of threatening or offensive language, including profanity, is not permitted.
  4. Do not send nonsense messages.

Communication is non-binding. When the communication phase ends, the decision stage begins wherein you are prompted to make your contribution choice.

You still receive 10 coins. The project is provided if total contributions reach 15 coins. If provided, contributions are doubled and shared equally. If not, contributions are lost.
